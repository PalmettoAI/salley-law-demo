## What to Lead With
> "I built a demo for Salley Law — you guys have a compelling story: personal injury on contingency, 25 years local, same-day response. But your current site isn't telling that story in a way that converts. This demo does."

## Cold-Call Talking Points
1. **Personal injury on contingency** — "No fees unless we win" is one of the highest-converting hooks in legal marketing. The demo leads with this.
2. **5-minute response system** — The demo highlights their speed-to-lead as a competitive advantage. First firm to respond almost always wins the PI case.
3. **Criminal defense** — DUI and drug cases are urgent, high-intent searches. Someone arrested Friday night is Googling immediately. The site needs to be built for that.
4. **Family law** — Divorce and custody clients are emotionally charged and do extensive research. A compassionate, well-designed site wins.
5. **The "answers the phone" angle** — This firm differentiates on accessibility. The demo is built around that brand promise.

## Pain Points to Address
- No meta description or schema = Google doesn't surface them for "personal injury attorney Lexington SC"
- PI cases are time-sensitive — a site with a fast intake form captures leads that would otherwise go to a competitor
- The after-hours availability is a massive selling point that most firm sites never communicate

## Key Differentiators
- PI on contingency (no fees unless you win)
- 25 years in Lexington County specifically
- Same-day attorney response (not paralegal)
- After-hours availability
